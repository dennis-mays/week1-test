About Me

My name is Dennis and I have over 30 years of IT support and management experience. During that time I have done everything from technical support to designing a network solution for a multi-million dollar company. I've also been project lead on multiple system and infrustructure upgrades.

I also have a creative side as well. Part of my duties included working with our marketing team. In that role I was responsible for producing product catalogs and posters that would be used at various trade shows and retail stores across the country. I also helped with the production of product photography that would be used in various brochures and websites as well as producing weekly promotional emails.

Lastly I was part of a team that reviewed and tested ecommerce websites for design and ease of use.
